###### A.6.1.1.7.2.6.1.2.2.3.3 - Burn USDS [Core]

Once liquidity has been recovered to the Osero ALM Proxy, the recovered USDS is repaid and burned through the USDS Facet, as specified in [A.2.2.10.1.1.1.5.2.2 - Burn USDS](f01e63b7-dde7-422a-89a1-6931839d49f5). USDS burning is unlimited so that the full outstanding amount can be burned.
