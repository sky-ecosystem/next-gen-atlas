###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.2.5.2 - Check Rate Limits [Core]

The swap is subject to the rate limit identified by `LIMIT_USDS_TO_USDC`. The amount swapped cannot exceed the amount permitted by this rate limit.
