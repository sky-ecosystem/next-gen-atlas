###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.3.1.6.3.2.4.1 - Deposit Rate Limits [Core]

The deposit rate limits are:

- `maxAmount`: 25,000,000 RLUSD or USDC
- `slope`: 25,000,000 RLUSD or USDC per day
