###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.1.2.3.2.7 - Swap USDC To DAI Iteratively If Needed [Core]

If `direct swap` is not possible, the operator must perform an `iterative swap` of USDC to DAI with DAI `refilling`.
