###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.2.4.2 - Check Rate Limits [Core]

The burn is subject to the rate limit identified by `LIMIT_USDS_BURN`. The amount burned cannot exceed the amount permitted by this rate limit.
