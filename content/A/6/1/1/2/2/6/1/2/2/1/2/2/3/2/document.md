###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.2.3.2 - Check Rate Limits [Core]

The mint is subject to the rate limit identified by `LIMIT_USDS_MINT`. The amount minted cannot exceed the amount permitted by this rate limit.
