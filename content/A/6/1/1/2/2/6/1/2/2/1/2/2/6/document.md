###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.2.6 - Swap USDC To USDS [Core]

The documents herein define the steps to swap USDC held by the ALM Proxy for USDS through the PSM. The swap is performed by calling the Diamond PAU Controller, specifying the amount of USDC to swap; the Controller dispatches the call to the PSM Facet.
