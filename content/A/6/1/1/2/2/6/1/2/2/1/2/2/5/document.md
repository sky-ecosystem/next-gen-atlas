###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.2.5 - Swap USDS To USDC [Core]

The documents herein define the steps to swap USDS held by the ALM Proxy for USDC through the PSM. The swap is performed by calling the Diamond PAU Controller, specifying the amount of USDC to receive; the Controller dispatches the call to the PSM Facet.
