###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.1.2.2 - ERC-4626 Functions [Core]

The documents herein define the general Grove Liquidity Layer operational procedures for interacting with ERC4626-compliant tokenized vaults. ERC4626 is a standard interface for vaults representing shares of an underlying ERC20 token. The Grove Liquidity Layer can integrate with various ERC4626 vaults.
