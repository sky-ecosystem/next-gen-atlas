###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.3.1.6.4.2.4.3 - Swap Rate Limits [Core]

The swap rate limits are:

- `maxAmount`: 5,000,000 USDC/AUSD
- `slope`: 100,000,000 USDC/AUSD per day
- `maxSlippage`: 0.1%
