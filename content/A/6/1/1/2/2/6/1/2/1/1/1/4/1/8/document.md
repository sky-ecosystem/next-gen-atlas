###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.1.1.1.4.1.8 - PSM Facet Contract [Core]

The address of the PSM Facet contract is: `0xE4A5dAc768a310cc2316f258901b32E499653064`. The PSM Facet contract implements the USDS and USDC swap operations through the PSM, as specified in [A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.2 - Diamond PAU Controller Functions](6c060c28-5619-48f4-8536-b74c153b1641). These swaps require the ALM Proxy to be whitelisted on the litePSM, as specified in [A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.1.1.4.2 - Whitelisting Of ALM Proxy](6823cc5a-6667-4754-a030-9ac7126b006e).
