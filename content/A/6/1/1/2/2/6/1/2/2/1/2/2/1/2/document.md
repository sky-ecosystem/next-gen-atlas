###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.2.1.2.2.1.2 - Check Rate Limits [Core]

The deposit is subject to the deposit rate limit identified by `LIMIT_BASIN_DEPOSIT` for the specified asset and Basin. The deposit cannot exceed the amount permitted by this rate limit.
