###### A.6.1.1.2.2.6.1.2.1.1.1.4 - Diamond PAU Contracts [Core]

The documents herein define the addresses of the Diamond Parallelized Allocation Unit (Diamond PAU) contracts for the Grove Liquidity Layer. The Diamond PAU is a modular implementation of the Allocation System in which a single Controller contract dispatches operations to specialized facet contracts, with integration configurations held in a Beacon contract.
