###### A.6.1.1.2.2.2.2.2.1.2.1.4 - Root Edit Token Holder Vote [Core]

Where their review of the proposal results in a finding of alignment with the Sky Core Atlas and Grove Artifact, the Operational Facilitator next triggers a Snapshot poll to allow token holders to vote on the proposal. The poll is open for three (3) days. A poll must have at least 10% of the circulating token supply participating and must have more than 50% of votes cast, excluding abstentions, in favor to be approved.
