###### A.6.1.1.4.2.5.1.4.4.4.5.0.6.1 - Payment Details Per Reward Period [Active Data]

The payment details consist of a table where each entry represents a single payment. Each entry has the following fields:

- Reward Period
- Payee
- Payment Address
- Amount Paid
- Transaction Hash
- Transaction Date
