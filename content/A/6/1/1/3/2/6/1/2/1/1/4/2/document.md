###### A.6.1.1.3.2.6.1.2.1.1.4.2 - Whitelisting Of ALMProxy [Core]

The ALMProxy for Keel is whitelisted on the LitePSM. This allows Keel to call `buyGemNoFee` and `sellGemNoFee` on the `MCD_LITE_PSM_USDC_A` contract.
