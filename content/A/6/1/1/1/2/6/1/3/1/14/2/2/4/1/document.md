###### A.6.1.1.1.2.6.1.3.1.14.2.2.4.1 - TransferAssets Rate Limits [Core]

The transferAssets rate limits are:

- `maxAmount`: 5,000,000 USDT
- `slope`: 100,000,000 USDT per day
- `maxSlippage`: 0.2%
- `rechargeRate`: 50,000 USDT per day
