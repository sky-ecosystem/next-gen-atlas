###### A.6.1.1.1.3.5.3.2.3.2 - Spark Savings USDT on Ethereum [Core]

The Rewards Rate for Spark Savings USDT on Ethereum shall be determined pursuant to the governance-approved Spark Savings rate-setting framework and applicable operational parameters approved by Spark governance. The applicable operational actor designated under the Spark governance framework may determine the Rewards Rate within the governance-approved operational bounds and Onchain Parameters (including the Min Yield and Max Yield for the vault).
