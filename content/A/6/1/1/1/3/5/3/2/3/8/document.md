###### A.6.1.1.1.3.5.3.2.3.8 - Spark Savings USDT on X Layer [Core]

The Rewards Rate for Spark Savings USDT on X Layer is set via the vault’s setter role in accordance with the Rewards Rate Operational Process ([A.6.1.1.1.3.5.3.2.2](6c7a4964-485f-4edf-a05f-61fa65c9871c)), within the Min Yield and Max Yield bounds specified in its Onchain Parameters ([A.6.1.1.1.3.5.2.2.8](c6eb9203-a5af-4f9b-baaf-b70c4449d4a4)). Within those bounds, the rate may be set as a fixed value or by reference to an external benchmark, as provided in the Rewards Rate Definition ([A.6.1.1.1.3.5.3.2.1](3a143911-80c9-4eb0-9aa3-5b3d3a8ca843)).
