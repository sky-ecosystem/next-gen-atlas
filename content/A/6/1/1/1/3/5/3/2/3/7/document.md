###### A.6.1.1.1.3.5.3.2.3.7 - Spark Savings USDT on Arbitrum [Core]

The Rewards Rate for Spark Savings USDT on Arbitrum is set via the vault’s setter role in accordance with the Rewards Rate Operational Process ([A.6.1.1.1.3.5.3.2.2](6c7a4964-485f-4edf-a05f-61fa65c9871c)), within the Min Yield and Max Yield bounds specified in its Onchain Parameters ([A.6.1.1.1.3.5.2.2.7](5a2b0ec2-5358-4464-b6cb-0d39642a437d)). Within those bounds, the rate may be set as a fixed value or by reference to an external benchmark, as provided in the Rewards Rate Definition ([A.6.1.1.1.3.5.3.2.1](3a143911-80c9-4eb0-9aa3-5b3d3a8ca843)).
