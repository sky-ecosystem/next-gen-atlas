###### A.6.1.1.1.3.7.2.4.2.2 - Borrow Rate [Core]

The minimum borrow rate for loans via the Arkis infrastructure must be the greater of the following at the time of issuance or renewal:

- Sky Savings Rate + 2%
- SOFR + 2.5%
