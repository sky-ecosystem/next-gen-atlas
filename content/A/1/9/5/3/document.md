###### A.1.9.5.3 - Protego [Core]

Protego is a contract that allows Sky Governance to cancel the execution of planned governance actions that are awaiting the expiration of the Governance Security Module Pause Delay (GSM Pause Delay). See [A.1.9.3.1 - Pause Delay](3c9545d9-775f-4149-88bf-7d297b5302c6). The subdocuments herein specify the features of the Protego contract and its authorized use.
