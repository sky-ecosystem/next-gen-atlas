###### A.1.9.2.4.8.4.2 - Core Facilitator Verifies Information In Executive Document [Core]

The Core Facilitator must make sure that the Executive Document is accurate and adheres to the specified conventions. The Core Facilitator must ensure that all the steps outlined in [A.1.9.2.4.8.3 - Core Facilitator Creates Executive Document](6d40ab22-bcf5-42c2-8a27-fef39fa43d6f) are followed.
