###### A.1.9.2.4.9.3 - Process For Handling Technical Issues [Core]

The Reviewers play a key role in the process for identifying, analyzing and resolving technical issues like bugs, mistakes and exploits when they arise during the Spell development process. Other stakeholders, including the Spell Crafter and Core Facilitators, also play an important role. The technical issue process is outlined here [A.1.9.2.4.7.3 - Process For Handling Technical Issues](2c8e81bf-b79a-486f-80eb-bd9b15464695). All technical issues identified by Reviewers should follow the steps specified in this process
