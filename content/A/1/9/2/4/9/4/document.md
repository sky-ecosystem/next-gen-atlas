###### A.1.9.2.4.9.4 - Spell Team Operational Principles [Core]

As a core member of the Spell Team, the Reviewers must comply with the Spell Team Operational Principles throughout their involvement in the Spell development process. These principles apply to both Spell crafting and Spell reviewing processes, and are outlined here [A.1.9.2.4.7.4 - Spell Team Operational Principles](bd45083c-50de-47d2-b0c3-808c04f726e3).
