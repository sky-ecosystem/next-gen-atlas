###### A.1.9.2.5.4 - Voting Outcome [Core]

The voting outcome is determined by the amount of SKY vote-weight allocated to the Executive Vote. If a proposal does not receive more SKY vote-weight than the current hat, it will not be scheduled for execution and will eventually expire; SKY holders can continue supporting it or redirect their vote-weight to another proposal, often the previously approved one, to maintain the status quo. For details of the execution process see [A.1.9.2.4.13 - Spell Execution Process And Retro (Step 13)](761cd866-17a9-47f5-8ae6-ab1788371be1).
