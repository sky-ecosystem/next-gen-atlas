###### A.1.11.2.7.1 - Language Disallowing Simultaneous Edits Not Allowed [Core]

Atlas Edit Proposals cannot include language that aims to prevent other Atlas Edit Proposals from editing the same component of the Atlas within the same Governance Cycle.
