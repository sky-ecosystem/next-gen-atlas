###### A.1.11.2.5 - Process Definition [Core]

The first Monday of each calendar month marks the beginning of the Monthly Governance Cycle.

Time is inclusive and based on UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) and the Gregorian Calendar.

**First Wednesday after the first Monday of the month:**

- The Authors of Atlas Edit Proposals must formally submit their AEP before this Day.
- The Formal Submission must be done through a message on the Author’s Forum thread that contains the AEP.

**Week 1, Friday**
After reviewing the AEPs, the Core Facilitator must decide whether each submitted AEP warrants moving forward to a Ratification Poll.

**Week 2, Monday**
The Core Facilitator publish the set of Ratification Polls. The format of these is defined in [A.1.11.2.6 - Ratification Poll Requirements](13e6da57-ee8e-4593-90f2-698642b1c82f).

Ratification Polls are published to the community GitHub, submitted on-chain, and appear on the official Voting Portal.

**Week 4, Monday**
The Ratification Polls conclude, and each proposal or set of proposals is marked as either Accepted or Rejected by the Core Facilitator.
