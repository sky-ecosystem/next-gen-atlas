###### A.1.11.2.1.7.2.0.4.1 - Amended - Blocked AEPs Cannot Be Re-Submitted In Original Form [Action Tenet]

An AEP that was blocked for misalignment cannot be resubmitted in its original form; it must be edited before it can be formally submitted again to the Monthly Cycle.
