###### A.1.11.2.1.7.2 - Resubmitting A Blocked AEP To A Future Monthly Cycle [Core]

An Atlas Edit Proposal that has been blocked for misalignment can be amended and re-submitted in a future Atlas Edit Monthly Governance Cycle.
