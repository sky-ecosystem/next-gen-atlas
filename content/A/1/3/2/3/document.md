###### A.1.3.2.3 - Correction Of Non-Conformance [Core]

Where the parties specified in [A.1.3.2.2 - Review Obligation](907407a8-123f-47bd-a120-9ce8f15c6c48) identify non-conformance between modifications to Synome Documents and the Atlas Documents, they must promptly report the non-conformance to the Synome Editor, and the Synome Editor must promptly correct it.
