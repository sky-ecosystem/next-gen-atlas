###### A.1.3.2.5 - Removal Of Designation [Core]

If Core GovOps determines that the Synome Editor is in misalignment as specified in [A.0.1.1.11 - Misalignment](60066e0f-3db7-45c7-9664-8120a4fbd38b), Core GovOps must promptly propose removal of the designation specified in [A.1.3.2.1.1 - Designated Synome Editor](d43f9ebf-49f6-4351-aaba-0e8b0a52d288) through the standard Atlas Edit Process.
