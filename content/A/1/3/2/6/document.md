###### A.1.3.2.6 - Emergency Pause [Core]

The Core Facilitator may pause the designation specified in [A.1.3.2.1.1 - Designated Synome Editor](d43f9ebf-49f6-4351-aaba-0e8b0a52d288) on an emergency basis pending consideration of removal as specified in [A.1.3.2.5 - Removal Of Designation](19305a42-0073-4934-b968-6a94c07fc969). While the designation is paused, modifications to Synome Documents made by the Synome Editor do not take effect.
