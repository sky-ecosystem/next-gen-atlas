###### A.1.2.2.1 - Atlas Document Type Categories [Core]

The Atlas Document Types can be broadly categorized into distinct groups that determine how they are modified and how they function in the Atlas. At the highest level there are the Immutable Documents and the Adaptive Documents. The Adaptive Documents are further subdivided into three (3) distinct groups: Primary Documents, Supporting Documents, and Accessory Documents. The category of an Atlas Document can be inferred from its Document Identifier, as each group has unique and non-overlapping rules for validity of their Document Identifiers.
