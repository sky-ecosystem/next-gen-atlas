###### A.1.2.2.1.0.4.1 - Determine How They Are Modified - Immutable Documents Can Be Amended In The Transition To Endgame [Action Tenet]

Currently, in the transition to Endgame, the Immutable Documents (Articles and Sections) can be amended pursuant to [A.1.10 - Weekly Governance Cycle](83edd4e1-692e-4566-a415-b8f272c33c5e) and [A.1.11 - Monthly Governance Cycle](7f2ba62c-9b3b-4df6-aa16-189a749cffa3)[**.**](7f2ba62c-9b3b-4df6-aa16-189a749cffa3) Once the Sky Ecosystem enters the Endgame State, the Immutable Documents of the Atlas will be forever locked down and cannot be changed.
