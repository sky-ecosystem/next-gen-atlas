###### A.1.2.2.1.1 - Immutable Document Category [Core]

Immutable Documents are the most important part of the Atlas: they record the Spirit of the Atlas, detailing the vision, purpose and unalienable principles of Sky. In the transition to Endgame State, Immutable documents remain modifiable by the governance processes specified elsewhere in the Atlas. Once the Endgame State has been reached, however, Immutable Documents become fully immutable, i.e., they can never be changed. Immutable Documents have Document identifiers that are at most three (3) layers deep in the Document Tree.
