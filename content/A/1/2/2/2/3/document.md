###### A.1.2.2.2.3 - The Scope Type [Type Specification]

[See below]

**Components**:

"Content": The content Component is very flexible and defines in broad human-readable language core principles, rules and processes.

**Doc Identifier Rules**:

Scope Documents have the Document Identifiers from A.1 to A.6.

**Additional Logic**:

Scope Documents are immutable, foundational parts of the Atlas and their content must be considered when interpreting all principles, rules and processes of Atlas Documents nested below them

**Type Category**:

Immutable Document

**Type Name**:

Scope

**Type Overview**:

The Scope Type is used for the six nonzero Immutable Documents of the first layer of the Atlas that directly describe focus areas, principles, rules and processes of Sky Governance. Scope Documents define the broad boundaries, requirements and objectives of each of the 5 Atlas Scopes, so that they together can fully cover all activities that are needed for Sky to function while maintaining its resilient equilibrium.
