###### A.1.2.1.2.1 - Name Property [Core]

The Name property is assigned to each Atlas Document to provide a human-readable name, making it easier for humans to identify the document and understand its purpose and function at a glance. This Name serves as an abstracted overview of the document's content, distinguishing it from other documents in a user-friendly manner.

However, for data management and record-keeping purposes, the official designation of an Atlas Document is its unique Document Identifier. As a result, the Name property can be modified with no substantive consequences.
