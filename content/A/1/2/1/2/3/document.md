###### A.1.2.1.2.3 - Last Modified Property [Core]

The Last Modified property specifies the exact date and time when the Atlas Document was updated to its current version. It is recorded in the UTC timezone and follows the format 'YYYY-MM-DD-HH:MM:SS'. This property provides a timestamp for tracking modifications and ensuring transparency in the governance and update process. When an Atlas Document is at version 1, the Last Modified property denotes when the Atlas Document was created.
