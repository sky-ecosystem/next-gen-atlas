###### A.1.2.1.2.4 - Type Property [Core]

The Type property of Atlas Documents specifies the function, characteristic and purpose of the Atlas Document. The Type determines the data that must be contained in the Components Property. The various Document Types and their characteristics are specified in [A.1.2.2 - Structure, Categories, And Types Of Atlas Documents](f65c083f-021b-4a12-b000-6764e4fe28c3).
