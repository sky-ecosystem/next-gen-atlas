###### A.1.13.4.7.1 - GovOps And Facilitators Acting As Executor Agents [Core]

In the short term, before the launch of Executor Agents, all references to Executor Agents in the Atlas refer to a GovOps Actor and a Facilitator, collectively. Sky Governance designates these actors as an Executor Agent on a transitional basis, and they have all the powers of Executor Agents as defined in the Atlas. When an Executor Agent enters into an Executor Accord during this transitionary period, it requires the mutual agreement of both the GovOps actor and the Facilitator, in addition to the Prime Agent.
