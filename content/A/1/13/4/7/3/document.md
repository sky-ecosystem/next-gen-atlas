###### A.1.13.4.7.3 - Full Launch Of Executor Agents [Core]

In the long term, Executor Agents will be fully launched with their own tokens and governance processes. At this point, GovOps actors and Facilitators will be contracted by Executor Agents through a vote of Executor Agent token holders.
