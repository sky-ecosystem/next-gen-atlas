###### A.1.13.4.1 - The Role Of Executor Agents [Core]

Executor Agents play a critical role in implementing Prime Agent strategies, as defined by their Executor Agent Accord with a particular Agent. Executor Agents are tasked with operationalizing Prime Agent strategies, and are able to directly update Agent Artifacts where sufficiently specific logic exists to do so.
