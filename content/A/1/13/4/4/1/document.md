###### A.1.13.4.4.1 - Executor Agent Alignment Requirements [Core]

The requirement for Executor Agents to check every Prime Agent action or process for alignment with the Atlas is fundamental. It cannot be amended or altered by either Prime Agents or Executor Agents.
