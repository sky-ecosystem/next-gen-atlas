###### A.1.13.4.5 - Executor Agent Right Of Refusal [Core]

Executor Agents have the discretion to refuse to operationalize a Prime Agent’s update to their Agent Artifact, or any other action, if they consider that the action is either misaligned with the Atlas or presents significant risk beyond the Executor Agent’s insured capacity.
