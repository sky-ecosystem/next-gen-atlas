###### A.1.13.4.6.1.1 - Executor Agent GovOps [Core]

GovOps actors carry out operational activities on behalf of Executor Agents and, by extension, Prime Agents that Executor Agents have entered into Executor Accords with. With the exception of specific activities reserved for Executor Agent Facilitators, all activities of Executor Agents are carried out by GovOps.
