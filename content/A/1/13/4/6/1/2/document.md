###### A.1.13.4.6.1.2 - Executor Agent Facilitators [Core]

Executor Agent Facilitators are Facilitators that carry out specific activities on behalf of Executor Agents. Whenever an Executor Agent must interpret an Artifact, that interpretation must be done by the Facilitator. Whenever an Executor Agent has privileged access to smart contracts of Sky or an Agent, that access must be held by the Facilitator. Facilitators use this access to carry out the Executor Agent’s operational responsibilities as directed by GovOps, unless the Facilitator believes that GovOps is misaligned.
