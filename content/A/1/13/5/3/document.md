###### A.1.13.5.3 - End Of Agent Termination Process [Core]

Once the Executor Agent has ensured that all operational activities and functions of the Prime Agent have been wound down, the Sky Core Facilitators must issue a notice in the Sky Forum indicating the formal termination of the Agent. Any residual assets or holdings of the Prime Agents, that could not be disbursed to Prime Agent token holders, revert to ownership by Sky Core.
