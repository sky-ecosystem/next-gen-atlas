###### A.1.13.1.5.4 - Emergency Process For Misaligned Agent Artifacts [Core]

In an emergency situation (see [A.1.8.1.1 - Definition Of Emergency Situations](5eafb29e-84a0-4a53-a798-3f958c880225)), Sky Core has the discretion to immediately suspend an Agent Artifact provision or function if it reasonably suspects that the Artifact is contributing towards a significant or system-level risk. Sky Core must act in good faith when exercising its discretion in emergency situations.
