###### A.1.13.2.5 - Agent Artifact Omni Documents [Core]

All Agent Artifacts contain Omni Documents. Omni Documents can be used to encode any operational or business logic that either spans multiple Sky Primitives, or relates to non-Sky Primitive infrastructure inherited from Sky Core or other activities unrelated to Sky Primitives.
