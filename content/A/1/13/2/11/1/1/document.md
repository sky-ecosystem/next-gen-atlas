###### A.1.13.2.11.1.1 - Changes By Agents [Core]

When Agents carry out changes in specifications themselves, each Agent must update their Agent Artifact through the appropriate governance process to conform to the new specifications.

Agents must carry out these changes within thirty (30) days of the change in specifications in the Atlas unless otherwise specified by Core GovOps.
