###### A.1.13.2.1 - Jurisdiction Of Agent Artifacts [Core]

Subject to any relevant provisions in the Sky Core Atlas, Agents are governed by the provisions of their respective Agent Artifacts. Agent Artifacts define the Agent’s strategic vision and day-to-day operational logic, including, in the case of Prime Agents, specific parameters or operational instructions relevant to the Agents’ business activities. These Artifacts play a pivotal role in delineating the operational processes that must be carried out by the Executor Agents with whom Prime Agents partner.
