###### A.1.13.2.2 - Interaction Of Agent Artifacts [Core]

Agent Artifacts must not limit or constrain the effect or operation of other Agent Artifacts. Agents can freely experiment or pursue divergent governance and operational logic within their respective Artifacts, subject to the fundamental requirement that all Artifacts remain aligned with the Sky Core Atlas.
