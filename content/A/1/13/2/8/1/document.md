###### A.1.13.2.8.1 - Founder Access Discretion [Core]

Prior to the Invocation and codification of the Prime Transformation or Executor Transformation Primitive within an Agent Artifact, the Agent’s Founder may unilaterally create new Omni Documents or edit existing Omni Documents.
