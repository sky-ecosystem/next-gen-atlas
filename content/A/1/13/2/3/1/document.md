###### A.1.13.2.3.1 - Canonical Version Of Agent Artifacts [Core]

While the text of Agent Artifacts may be copied and made accessible in other locations, the version of a given Agent Artifact publicly documented within the Agent Scope of the Sky Atlas must be treated as canonical. In the event of any discrepancies between the Agent Artifact recorded in the Sky Atlas and elsewhere, the version formally documented in the Sky Atlas shall prevail.
