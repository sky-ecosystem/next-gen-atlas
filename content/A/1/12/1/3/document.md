###### A.1.12.1.3 - Update Process [Core]

The Active Data Update Process specified in any Active Data Controller document must conform to one of the processes defined in the subdocuments herein.

Since the Update Process element is defined in an Active Data Controller Document, modification of that Update Process element is subject to a SKY vote pursuant to standard governance processes.
