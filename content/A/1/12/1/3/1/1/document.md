###### A.1.12.1.3.1.1 - Corrections By Core Facilitator [Core]

To correct a clear error, the Core Facilitator may directly update an Active Data document at any time. Prior to taking such action, the Core Facilitator should notify the respective Responsible Party charged with maintaining the Active Data document.

However, if the error or issue is not clearly evident, the Active Data document must be updated in accordance with the established procedure defined in [A.1.12.1.3.1 - Direct Edit](ecce1a73-dac3-4fe5-a9d6-8b445bbc591a).
