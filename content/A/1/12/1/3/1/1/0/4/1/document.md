###### A.1.12.1.3.1.1.0.4.1 - Clear Error [Action Tenet]

The Target Document grants Facilitators the authority to directly edit an Active Data document if it contains a mistake or inaccuracy that is obvious, unambiguous, and indisputable. The term "clear error" means that the mistake is apparent and does not require extensive debate or investigation to be recognized as such.
