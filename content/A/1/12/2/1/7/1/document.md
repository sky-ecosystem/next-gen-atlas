###### A.1.12.2.1.7.1 - Procedure For Blocking AEP For Misalignment [Core]

An Atlas Edit Proposal can be blocked by the Core Facilitator for misalignment.

Where an AEP has been blocked for misalignment, the Core Facilitator must update the AEP status as "Rejected-Misaligned".

The Core Facilitator should issue a written advisory opinion containing their findings/reasoning for blocking the AEP.
