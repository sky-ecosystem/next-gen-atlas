###### A.1.1.2.0.3.1 - Extrapolated - Element Annotation [Annotation]

The element "extrapolated" refers to the process by which a Facilitator has inferred or deduced from their understanding of the Spirit of the Atlas a necessary course of action when explicit guidance is not available. This inferred action is also based on the Facilitator’s current state of Universal Alignment.
