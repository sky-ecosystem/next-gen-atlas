###### A.1.10.1.3.0.3.3 - Upcoming Executive Vote - Element Annotation [Annotation]

Successfully polled changes to the Sky Protocol are included in an Executive Vote that is typically conducted within approximately 30 days of the poll passing. The exact timing is dependent on the decisions made by the Core Facilitator and the spell teams.
