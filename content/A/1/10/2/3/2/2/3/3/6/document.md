###### A.1.10.2.3.2.2.3.3.6 - Prime Agent Delivers Prime Spell Payload [Core]

By Friday, 16:00 UTC of week 2, the Prime Agent must deliver the final Prime Spell payload in the govops channel in Slack. The delivery must include the Prime Spell address, codehash, and execution type. The execution type must be specified in accordance with [A.1.10.2.3.2.3 - Execution Of Agent Spells](5b0fd894-094e-4b0c-91ca-c5b59b710c0c).
