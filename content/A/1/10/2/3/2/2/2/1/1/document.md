###### A.1.10.2.3.2.2.2.1.1 - Forum Post By Prime Agent [Core]

The technical implementation of Interim Deployments must successfully complete all required external audits as specified in [A.1.10.2.7 - Audit Process](a8334e19-fedb-48fb-a38c-f18666ccc8e6) before the Prime can post its Interim Deployment proposal to the Forum.
