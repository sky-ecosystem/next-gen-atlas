###### A.1.10.2.3.2.2.3.1.1 - Prime Agent Submits Prime Spell Form [Core]

The Prime Agent submits a Prime Spell Form to the Executive Process Liaison by Monday, 16:00 UTC of week 0. The Prime Spell Form initiates the Prime Spell Process for the upcoming Executive Vote cycle and specifies the proposed Prime Spell content. The Prime Agent must include the complexity score calculation for the proposed Prime Spell content in the Prime Spell Form. Items that introduce new code must have a completed audit as specified in [A.1.10.2.7 - Audit Process](a8334e19-fedb-48fb-a38c-f18666ccc8e6) before submission.
