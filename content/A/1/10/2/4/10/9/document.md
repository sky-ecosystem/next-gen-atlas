###### A.1.10.2.4.10.9 - Spell Crafter Hands Over Spell [Core]

Once the Spell Crafter has received approval to handover the Spell from both the Spell Reviewers, the Spell Crafter proceeds to hand over the Spell to the Governance Point. These steps are set out in the Core Spell Crafter Mainnet Workflow, as specified in [A.1.10.2.5.1.3.2.0.6.1 - Registered Spell Checklists](93f5b36b-06a7-4282-9fd7-14e0cbafd08e).
