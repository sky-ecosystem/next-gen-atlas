###### A.1.10.2.4.10.1 - Spell Crafter Performs Pre-Deployment Steps [Core]

After the Spell Reviewers have finished their review, the Spell Crafter performs the last steps before the Spell is ready to be deployed. These steps are set out in the Core Spell Crafter Mainnet Workflow, as specified in [A.1.10.2.5.1.3.2.0.6.1 - Registered Spell Checklists](93f5b36b-06a7-4282-9fd7-14e0cbafd08e).
