###### A.1.10.2.4.10.10 - Spell Crafter Conducts Final Checks [Core]

The Spell Crafter conducts a final set of checks to guard against Pull Request attack vectors before merging the Pull Request as specified herein. These steps are set out in the Core Spell Crafter Mainnet Workflow, as specified in [A.1.10.2.5.1.3.2.0.6.1 - Registered Spell Checklists](93f5b36b-06a7-4282-9fd7-14e0cbafd08e).
