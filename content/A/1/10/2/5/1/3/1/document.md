###### A.1.10.2.5.1.3.1 - Spell Checklist Registration Requirements [Core]

Each entry in the Spell Checklists Registry must contain the following information:

- Name
- Type (Core Spell Checklist, Agent Spell Checklist, or Operational Checklist)
- URL
