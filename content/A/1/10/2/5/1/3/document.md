###### A.1.10.2.5.1.3 - Spell Checklists Registry [Core]

All Spell Checklists must be recorded in the Spell Checklists Registry at [A.1.10.2.5.1.3.2.0.6.1 - Registered Spell Checklists](93f5b36b-06a7-4282-9fd7-14e0cbafd08e). Checklists specified in [A.1.10.2.5.2 - Operational Reference Materials](642c4577-3d39-4a05-a15a-94bdd7e09f50) that are used during the Spell process must also be recorded in the Spell Checklists Registry with the type Operational Checklist.
