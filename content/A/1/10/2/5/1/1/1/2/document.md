###### A.1.10.2.5.1.1.1.2 - Core Spell Reviewer Checklist [Core]

The Core Spell Reviewer Checklist defines the mandatory steps for internal and external Spell Reviewers. All Spell Reviewers must complete the Core Spell Reviewer Checklist for every Core Spell review.
