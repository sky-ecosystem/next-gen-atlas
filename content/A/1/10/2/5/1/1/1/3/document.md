###### A.1.10.2.5.1.1.1.3 - Module Onboarding Checklists [Core]

Module Onboarding Checklists define use-case specific steps that must be followed when a Core Spell includes the onboarding of a new module. Module Onboarding Checklists are created after the first onboarding of a given module type, based on the learnings from that onboarding. The absence of a Module Onboarding Checklist does not block the onboarding of a new module type.
