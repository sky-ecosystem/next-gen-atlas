###### A.1.10.2.5.2.2.2 - Deployment Checklist Update Process [Core]

The Deployment Checklist is maintained by Core GovOps. All changes must be submitted as Pull Requests to the Ecosystem Operational References repository (https://github.com/Atlas-Axis/ecosystem-operational-references) and require two (2) approvals, one (1) from each Core Spell Team, before they can be merged.
