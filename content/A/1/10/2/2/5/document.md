###### A.1.10.2.2.5 - Prime Agents [Core]

Prime Agents (see [A.0.1.1.42 - Prime Agent](a8454271-c090-4084-b022-4430e3def93c)) participate in the Executive Process by submitting their spell items for inclusion in the Executive Vote. A Prime Agent's spell items are the onchain changes that require a Spell to execute. These spell items are advanced through the Prime Spell Process, which is coordinated by the Executive Process Liaison (see [A.1.10.2.1.13 - Executive Process Liaison](abd8ac0d-1265-4a71-aaea-2fff6670eac5)) and scoped by the Strategic Team (see [A.1.10.2.1.14 - Strategic Team](f5decc3b-f22e-4ee8-a55d-d14eed218916)).
