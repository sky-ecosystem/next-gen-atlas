###### A.1.4.5.0.4.1.1.4 - Volunteer Discord Moderator [Scenario]

**Description**:

Entity was a volunteer moderator for Sky’s Discord. Without leaving their moderator role, Entity registered and became active as an Aligned Delegate.

**Finding**:

Aligned

**Additional Guidance**:

‘Volunteer Discord moderator' is not a role that is formally defined in the Atlas. Therefore, Entity's first position as volunteer Discord moderator does not count as an ecosystem role. The Target Document is not triggered at all in this Scenario.
