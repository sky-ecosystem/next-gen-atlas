###### A.1.14.1.5.1 - Core Facilitator Review [Core]

Once a Notice of Potential Conflict is posted on the Sky Forum, the Core Facilitator must review the merits of the claim. The Core Facilitator has the discretion to conduct an investigation and collect more information as needed to assess the potential conflict.
