###### A.2.9.1.1.2.4 - Lawyer Registry Template [Core]

- .x: [Advisor name and short description]
- .x.1: [Name of Firm]
- .x.2: [Specialization Area]
- .x.3: [Jurisdiction]
- .x.4: [RF qualified (y/n)]
