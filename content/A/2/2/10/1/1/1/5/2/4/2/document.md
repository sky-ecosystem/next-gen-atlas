###### A.2.2.10.1.1.1.5.2.4.2 - Check Rate Limits [Core]

The withdrawal is subject to the withdrawal rate limit identified by `LIMIT_AAVE_WITHDRAW` for the asset and market. The withdrawal cannot exceed the amount permitted by this rate limit.
