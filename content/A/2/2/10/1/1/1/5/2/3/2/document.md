###### A.2.2.10.1.1.1.5.2.3.2 - Check Rate Limits [Core]

The deposit is subject to the deposit rate limit identified by `LIMIT_AAVE_DEPOSIT` for the asset and market. The deposit cannot exceed the amount permitted by this rate limit.
