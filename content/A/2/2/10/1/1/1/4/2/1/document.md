###### A.2.2.10.1.1.1.4.2.1 - Aave Facet [Core]

The Aave Facet (`AaveFacet`) supplies and withdraws an underlying asset to and from an Aave v3 lending pool, or an Aave v3 fork such as SparkLend, holding the aTokens in the ALM Proxy. Its address on Ethereum Mainnet is `0x8CE890A96a193ff2DD4B2eA3C682326F655f6b62`.
