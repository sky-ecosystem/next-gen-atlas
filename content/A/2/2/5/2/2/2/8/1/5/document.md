###### A.2.2.5.2.2.2.8.1.5 - Compliance Deadline For Existing Prime Agents [Core]

Existing Prime Agents whose Root Edit Primitive does not already incorporate the requirements specified in [A.2.2.5.2.2.2.8.1 - Agent Termination Process](82f9f4b9-76db-4ad7-94af-200ffe7c3b75) must update their Agent Artifact to include them by September 1, 2026.
