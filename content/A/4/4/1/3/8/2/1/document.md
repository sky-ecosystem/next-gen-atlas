###### A.4.4.1.3.8.2.1 - Str Parameters [Core]

The stUSDS BEAM parameters for the `str` stUSDS parameter are as follows:

- `max` - 5,000 basis points,
- `min` - 200 basis points,
- `step` - 1,500 basis points.
