###### A.3.2.2.1.3.1.1 - Administrative Risk Rating Cap [Core]

The Administrative Risk Rating Cap $\text{CAP}$ is a temporary cap on the Administrative Risk Rating. The value of the $\text{CAP}$ is `30`.
