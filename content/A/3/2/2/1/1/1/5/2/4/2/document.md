###### A.3.2.2.1.1.1.5.2.4.2 - Calculate Aggregate Risk Weighted Assets [Core]

Aggregate RWA is calculated as the sum of (1) Credit RWA, (2) Market RWA, (3) Counterparty Credit RWA, and (4) Credit Valuation Adjustment RWA.
