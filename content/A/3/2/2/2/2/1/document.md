###### A.3.2.2.2.2.1 - External Per Internal (EPI) Ratio Value [Core]

The External Per Internal (EPI) ratio is **1.00**. For example, if a Prime Agent has $20 million of Internal Junior Risk Capital, the EPI ratio enables it to source $20 million of External Junior Risk Capital.

This EPI ratio governs the initial mechanism for sourcing EJRC (SEJRC + TEJRC) and is based purely on the Prime’s IJRC; Primes can source EJRC via this initial mechanism up to a maximum amount equal to their IJRC holdings. However, additional EJRC may potentially be acquired via the alternative use of SPJ capacity, as described in [A.3.2.1.2.3.1.2.1 - Alternative Use Of SPJ Capacity To Source External Junior Risk Capital](03029174-91b4-4974-af1e-52438556a70b).
