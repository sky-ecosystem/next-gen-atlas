###### A.3.2.2.7.2.1.4 - Conservatorship For Breach Of Capital Requirements [Core]

In the event that less extreme measures are not adequate to address the capital shortfall, Core GovOps may seek to put the Prime Agent into conservatorship, in which case Sky Core Facilitators take direct control of the Prime Agent to maximize value for Sky and other Prime Agent stakeholders. Seeking to put a Prime Agent into conservatorship requires immediate escalation to Sky Core Governance and requires an expedited Executive Vote as specified in [A.1.8.1.5.4.1 - Known And Uncontentious Remedies](3f5f79fa-698c-42db-a941-ead5a5d0aa48).
