###### A.3.1.2.3.2 - Treatment Of USDS and Dai Balances [Core]

Prime Agents receive the full Agent Rate on Unrewarded USDS and Dai balances through the Monthly Settlement Cycle, as specified in [A.3.1.2.3.6 - Settlement](eed3d922-7bb8-4cee-97a4-47e902a1c937).
