###### A.3.7.1.6.5 - Genesis Agents [Core]

Genesis Agents are Agents that receive capital contributions from Sky, including, without limitation:

- Spark
- Grove
- Keel
- Skybase
- Obex
- Launch Agent 6
- Core Council Executor Agent 1
- Amatsu
- Ozone
