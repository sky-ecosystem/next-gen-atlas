###### A.0.1.1.24 - Stability Scope (STA) [Core]

The Stability Scope Alignment Artifact codifies the rules related to managing the core Stablecoin product, USDS, and supporting factors related to financial stability, such as surplus buffer and decentralized asset reserve.
