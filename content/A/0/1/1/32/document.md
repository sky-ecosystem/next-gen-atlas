###### A.0.1.1.32 - SKY Staking [Core]

When a user stakes their SKY tokens, they receive Staking Rewards. Staked SKY can be freely unstaked at any time. SKY Staking also allows users to borrow USDS against their SKY at the SKY Borrow Rate.
