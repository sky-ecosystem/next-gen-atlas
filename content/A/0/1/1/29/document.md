###### A.0.1.1.29 - Implicit Incentives [Core]

Implicit Incentives are the motivations or benefits that an actor can infer based on a ‘letter of the rules’ interpretation of an Alignment Artifact, and an assessment of the likelihood that these rules will be enforced.
