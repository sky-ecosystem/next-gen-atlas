###### A.0.1.1.21 - Governance Scope (GOV) [Core]

The Governance Scope Alignment Artifact codifies rules that regulate the critical balance-of-power processes defined in the Atlas, and adjudicate on appeals processes related to misalignment in the ecosystem.
