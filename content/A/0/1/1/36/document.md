###### A.0.1.1.36 - stUSDS [Core]

The token used to provide the capital for SKY Staking leverage is called stUSDS. It ensures that all SKY-backed borrowing is funded by segregated risk capital. Users can deposit USDS into a smart contract and receive stUSDS, which can later be converted back to USDS as long as there is unutilized USDS in the stUSDS converter smart contract.
