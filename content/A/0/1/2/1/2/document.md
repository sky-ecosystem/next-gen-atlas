###### A.0.1.2.1.2 - Precedence Over Conflicting Provisions [Core]

The broad interpretive and discretionary capacity granted by [A.0.1.2.1 - Core Council’s Broad Discretionary Capacity](f18229fe-fbc3-4dc8-ad84-4bca2915f6c4) takes precedence over any conflicting provisions in the Atlas.
