###### A.0.1.2.1 - Core Council’s Broad Discretionary Capacity [Core]

The next-generation Atlas is a self-contained governance infrastructure powering the Sky Ecosystem. It is designed to grow in tandem with the adaptive intelligence of the Ecosystem. Yet these first iterations of the next-generation Atlas may not sufficiently account for the critical, unforeseen contingencies that can arise, particularly as the Protocol is in a dynamic period of evolution.

To ensure the Ecosystem grows in resilience and advances toward the Endgame State, it must be able to respond quickly to pressing changes in its environment. To facilitate this, broad discretionary power is granted to the Core Council, given its close proximity to, and knowledge of, the Sky Ecosystem.

Where the Atlas does not provide clear guidance on a current challenge, the Core Facilitator, on behalf of the Core Council, is empowered to infer the Spirit of the Atlas in such a way as to maximize Universal Alignment and secure the Ecosystem’s growth, security and progression toward the Endgame State.

The Core Council may supersede existing Atlas provisions that it determines to be unsuitable for current challenges. Such supersession may be made through a public post by the Core Facilitator on the Sky Forum, confirmed by Core GovOps and the Core Council Risk Advisor.

The discretionary power granted herein is temporary.
